# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Customs brokers, freight forwarders, logistics teams, and import/export businesses that prepare delivery manifests for ASYCUDA submission. They are usually working against deadlines, juggling spreadsheets, and need a tool that is fast, trustworthy, and easy to explain to colleagues.

## Product Purpose

Convert Excel delivery manifests into ASYCUDA-compliant XML in under 60 seconds, with minimal friction and no file storage on disk. The product exists to reduce manual formatting, catch validation problems early, and let users move from spreadsheet to customs-ready output with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Calm, expert, precise.

The interface should feel competent rather than flashy: clear hierarchy, direct copy, and an air of reliability. Users should feel that the product understands customs workflows, values their time, and will not surprise them.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS marketing pages, neon startup gradients, decorative glass cards, over-rounded components, and anything that feels playful in a way that undermines trust. Do not imitate consumer-tech landing pages or clutter the experience with feature bloat, jargon, or visual noise.

## Design Principles

1. Make the main task obvious immediately.
2. Reduce cognitive load with clear structure and progressive disclosure.
3. Signal security, validation, and reliability at every step.
4. Prefer familiar product patterns over novelty.
5. Keep the interface efficient on desktop and fully usable on smaller screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain strong color contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic landmarks, and 44px touch targets. Keep motion subtle and respect reduced-motion preferences. Error messages should be plain English and actionable.
